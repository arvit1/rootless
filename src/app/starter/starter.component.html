Enough is enough

Let's face it. You may have had a tough life with sparse opportunities and many stereotypes. Maybe you've been labeled a few thing too... right? So have we! Enough is enough.

From rootless to rooted

That's why we call ourselves Rootless. Stereotypes, labels, stigmas, beliefs, upbringing be damned. From Rootless to Rooted, that's what it means!
